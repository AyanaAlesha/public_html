Summary

Software development is the process of designing, creating, testing, maintaining software applications, systems, and programs. It encompasses a wide range of activities, from conceptualization and planning to coding, testing, deployment, and ongoing maintenance.

Software developers can specialize in various areas, such as web development, mobile app development, game development, back-end, front-end, and more.
